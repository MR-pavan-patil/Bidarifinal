Our Committee Members

Vasantrao Patil

Designation: Vice-President

Mr. Vasantrao Patil is a seasoned journalist with decades of experience in the media industry. As Vice-President of the Bidari Cultural Forum, he has played a vital role in amplifying the voice of rural artists and preserving cultural heritage. His vision and communication expertise help connect the trust with the community and media at large.

Apparao Sowdi

Designation: General Secretary

Mr. Apparao Sowdi, a passionate journalist and committed social contributor, holds the position of General Secretary. He oversees internal coordination, manages event planning, and ensures smooth communication between departments. His integrity and dedication strengthen the organizational backbone of the trust.

Shyamala Patil

Designation: Trustee

Smt. Shyamala Patil is a renowned singer and cultural mentor who serves as a Trustee of the organization. She is instrumental in curating musical events, promoting women's participation in cultural programs, and nurturing young talent in the region. Her presence brings grace and depth to the trust's artistic mission.

About Bidari Utsav

Bidari Utsav is an annual cultural event held in Bidar, Karnataka, celebrating the rich history, music, and traditions of the region. Join us for a memorable experience!
